About Shivam Dairy

Shivam Dairy is a proud subsidiary of Shree Vareshwar Dudh Dairy (established in 2008), and was founded in 2021 with a vision to bring high-quality, fresh dairy products to local communities. The dairy was established in Jesar, a town in the Bhavnagar district of Gujarat, by Devkubhai Mala and his two dedicated partners.

Rooted in trust and tradition, Shivam Dairy carries forward the legacy of purity and authenticity. With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we strive to deliver farm-fresh, preservative-free milk and dairy products that families can rely on every day.
